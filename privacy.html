Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to SM LIMOUSINE. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our premium chauffeur and transportation services.

By using our services or submitting information through our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information solely for the purpose of providing our chauffeur and transportation services. This may include:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Pickup and destination locations
  • Flight details (airline, flight number, arrival/departure times)
  • Booking preferences (vehicle type, number of passengers, special requests)
  • Payment information necessary to process transactions

We collect this information when you make a reservation through our website, by phone, by email, or through a third-party booking platform.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to deliver and improve our services:

  • Managing and confirming your bookings
  • Communicating ride status, driver details, and pickup updates
  • Processing payments and issuing receipts
  • Improving service quality and customer experience
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared only with our trusted drivers and operational partners as necessary to fulfill your booking.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encrypted data transmission, secure storage systems, and restricted access protocols.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We continuously review and update our security practices to maintain the highest level of data protection.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Booking records are retained for a reasonable period to support repeat customers and handle any service inquiries.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of any non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
